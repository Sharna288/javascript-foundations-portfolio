- `/=`

- `===` Strict Equality

- `>`

- `>=`
- `<=`

### Logical Operators

- `&&` AND
- `||` OR
- `!` NOT

---

## Important Notes

Always prefer `===` and `!==` instead of `==` and `!=`.

The modulus operator `%` is commonly used to determine whether a number is even or odd.
